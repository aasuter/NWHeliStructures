Company Profile

Formed in May 2006, Northwest Heli Structures was founded by former employees of Helipro Corporation, now Vector Aerospace.

Our FAA Certified helicopter mechanics are true professionals, meeting our minimum requirements of:

- 10 years of experience in structural repair and modification of helicopters

- FAA Airframe/PP Certification

Our technicians are FAA Certified Mechanics, knowledgeable in a maintaining and modifying a wide variety of helicopters.

As an independent helicopter repair and modification contractor, we are able to offer our capabilities globally and tailor our service to meet the needs of our diverse clientele.
